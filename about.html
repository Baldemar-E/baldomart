Who?

Born and raised in San Antonio, Texas, Baldemar, grew up with a love for music so bad that he decided it was up to him to be the family embarassment and record his first hit single, ''Eating *ss'', in 2015.
A brief, but sweet number that would excite his high school class and set the trajectory for his launch into spaced out, home-recording madness.
One debut EP, some singles, and a million house shows later, the slightly seasoned, pot-head romantic, is currently bunkered at home keeping busy with projects and feeding his feeble mind with academic sustenance.
Baldemar looks forward to releasing new music and eventually getting back to gigs with his fellow goons, Damon, Adrian, and Nick Gucci.
But until then it's lonies til the ronie gonie!
