Here's a few great organizations to donate to and get involved with!

Color of Change

Support an online racial justice organization dedicated to fighting injustices against the Black community in America whether it's in elections, socio-economics, criminal justice or in the media. Color of Change is here to empower Black America. Visit their website to learn more and donate.

The Lilith Fund

This Texas non-profit works to make safe and legal abortions accessible to people of low-income. They provide the necessary financial and emotional support to help any person overcome the barriers inhibitibg their right to make their own reproductive choices. Donate to the Lilith Fund today.

RAICES

Providing free and low-cost legal services to immigrant youth, families, and refugees, RAICES, protects immigrant and refugee lives. They aim to create a safer, compassionate world for undocumented individuals that are mistreated and denied of their human rights. Support RAICES.

RAINN

Standing right beside victims, RAINN, is the largest anti-sexual assualt organization in the US. RAINN is committed to violence prevention, assisting survivors, and seeing that culprits are brought to justice. Their hotline can be reached at 800.656.HOPE at any time of need. Support, by donating.

The Sylvia Rivera Law Project

Named after the late civil rights piooner, SRLP, works to strenghten trans, inter-sex, & other gender non-conforming people agaisnt social injustices including discrimination and violence via legal services, policy reform and public education. Donate today!
